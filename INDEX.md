## 💡 Presentation Tips

### Before You Present

1. **Practice the Flow:** Rehearse the 15–20 minute delivery at least 3 times
2. **Know Your Numbers:** Be able to defend every metric and assumption
3. **Prepare Backup Data:** Have detailed financial model, customer feedback, and competitive analysis ready
4. **Test Technology:** Verify HTML files open correctly in target browser
5. **Have Demo Ready:** Open live product URL in separate browser tab
6. **Print Backup:** Have PDF version as backup (optional)

### During Presentation

1. **Start Strong:** Open with the $50B market opportunity (Slide 2)
2. **Show, Don't Tell:** Use live product demo during Slides 5–6
3. **Emphasize Traction:** Highlight MVP status and live deployment (Slide 7)
4. **Be Specific:** Use real pricing examples ($1,499 quote, $2,988 annual value)
5. **Address Timing:** Emphasize perfect storm of market conditions (Slide 12)
6. **Invite Questions:** Pause after each section for investor questions

### Handling Common Questions

**Q: "What's your competitive advantage?"**  
A: "We're the first-mover in turnover-specific SaaS. Competitors are either generic property management platforms or manual coordination. We've built a platform specifically designed for the turnover workflow."

**Q: "How will you acquire customers?"**  
A: "We're starting with mid-market property managers (50–500 units) via direct outreach and industry events. CAC is $500–$1K with 3–4 month payback. We'll expand to enterprise and franchises in Year 2."

**Q: "What's your go-to-market timeline?"**  
A: "Phase 1 (Months 1–6): 10–20 beta customers. Phase 2 (Months 7–12): 100+ customers, $50K+ MRR. Phase 3 (Year 2+): Enterprise expansion to 1,000+ unit portfolios."

**Q: "How will you handle competition?"**  
A: "We're building a moat through product specialization (turnover-specific), immutable audit trail (compliance), and network effects (contractors prefer one platform). By the time competitors notice, we'll have market share and customer lock-in."

**Q: "What's your profitability timeline?"**  
A: "Month 18 with conservative 30% MoM growth in Year 1. We'll hit 10% operating margin by Year 3 with 500+ customers and $2.1M ARR."
